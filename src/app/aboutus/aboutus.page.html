About Us

IoT Alliance is a not for profit club founded by Dr. Kayalvizhi Jayavel to offer internships on embedded systems in 2014. Later, to travel along the trend and strengthen, a team of students joined on board and started gaining and disseminating their expertise about Internet of Things (IoT), who are our pillars now driving the entire club, the alumni mentors. This club is redefining the faculty, alumni and junior connect to a new dimension. IoT Alliance has support from Department of Information Technology, SRM Institute of Science and Technology, Kattankalathur, Chennai. We are working on several projects few of which are completed and deployed successfully.
